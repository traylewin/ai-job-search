What We Look For:

  • 5+ years of relevant software engineering experience
  • Proficiency with at least one backend language (Python, Go, Java, etc.)
  • Experience building RESTful APIs and/or GraphQL services
  • Strong understanding of relational databases (PostgreSQL, MySQL)
  • Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
  • A growth mindset and willingness to learn new technologies
