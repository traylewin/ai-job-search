Senior Software Engineer

Department:Engineering - Runtime
Location:San Francisco, CA (Hybrid)
Employment Type:Full-time
Seniority Level:Senior
Requisition ID:ENG-2025-9928

About Databricks

Databricks is a leader in its space, serving thousands of customers worldwide. Our engineering team builds the infrastructure that powers modern cloud applications. We're a team of builders who care deeply about reliability, performance, and developer experience.

About the Team

The Runtime team is responsible for the core systems that power our platform. We handle billions of data points daily and build the tools that our customers rely on for mission-critical operations. You'll work alongside talented engineers who are passionate about distributed systems, observability, and building things that scale.

What You'll Do

  • Design, implement, and maintain large-scale distributed systems processing billions of events per day
  • Improve system reliability, performance, and operational efficiency
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams including Product, Design, and SRE
  • Write clean, well-tested, production-quality code
  • Contribute to technical strategy and architectural direction
  • Participate in code reviews and help maintain high engineering standards
  • Debug complex production issues across multiple services and infrastructure layers
  • Contribute to our engineering blog and speak at conferences (optional but encouraged)

Who You Are

  • You have 5-8+ years of experience building and shipping production software
  • You are proficient in one or more of: Go, Python, Java, Rust, C++
  • You have deep experience with distributed systems (consensus protocols, replication, partitioning)
  • You understand the tradeoffs between consistency, availability, and partition tolerance
  • You've operated services at scale and know the difference between "works in staging" and "works in production"
  • You're comfortable with ambiguity and can turn vague requirements into concrete technical plans
  • You communicate clearly in writing and verbally

Bonus Points

  • Experience with time-series databases or streaming systems
  • Contributions to open-source projects in the observability/monitoring space
  • Experience with Kubernetes and container orchestration at scale
  • Background in SRE or platform engineering

Compensation & Benefits

The base salary range for this position is $190,000 - $260,000 annually. In addition to base salary, this role is eligible for equity awards, annual bonus, and a comprehensive benefits package.

Our Benefits Include:

  • Competitive health, dental, and vision insurance
  • 401(k) plan with employer match
  • Generous PTO and company holidays
  • Parental leave (16 weeks primary, 8 weeks secondary)
  • Learning & development budget ($5,000/year)
  • Home office setup stipend
  • Monthly wellness stipend
  • Free lunch in office (3 days/week)
  • Employee stock purchase plan (ESPP)

Our Interview Process

  1. Recruiter phone screen (30 min)
  2. Technical phone screen with an engineer (60 min)
  3. Virtual onsite (4-5 hours): system design, coding, behavioral
  4. Team match / culture conversation
  5. Offer

Databricks is an equal opportunity employer. We celebrate diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive environment for all employees.
