About Robinhood

Robinhood is on a mission to democratize finance for all. We're looking for talented engineers to help us build the next generation of our trading platform.

 

About the Team

The Trading Platform team builds and maintains the core systems that power every trade on our platform. We handle millions of transactions daily with sub-millisecond latency requirements and strict regulatory compliance needs.

 

What You'll Do

  • Design and build low-latency trading systems
  • Work with market data feeds and order management systems
  • Ensure regulatory compliance in all engineering decisions
  • Optimize system performance for microsecond-level improvements
  • Build monitoring and alerting for critical financial systems

 

What You Bring

  • 5+ years of backend engineering experience
  • Experience with Python, Go, or Java
  • Knowledge of financial systems and/or trading platforms (preferred)
  • Experience with event-driven architectures and message queues
  • Strong understanding of concurrency and thread safety
