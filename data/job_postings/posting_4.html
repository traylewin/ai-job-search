Senior Backend Engineer

Collaboration Infrastructure · San Francisco, CA

Why this role matters

At Figma, design and engineering are deeply intertwined. We believe the best products come from teams where engineers and designers think together. As a Senior Backend Engineer, you'll build the systems that make real-time collaboration possible for millions of creators, designers, and product teams worldwide.

What you'll do

  • Build and scale the infrastructure powering real-time multiplayer collaboration
  • Design elegant APIs and data models for complex creative tools
  • Optimize performance for sub-100ms interactions at global scale
  • Work with CRDTs, operational transforms, and other collaboration primitives
  • Partner closely with product designers to prototype and ship features

Who we're looking for

  • 5+ years building production systems, ideally in collaboration or creative tools
  • Strong systems programming skills (we use C++, Rust, TypeScript)
  • Deep understanding of networking, concurrency, and distributed state
  • Experience with WebSocket-based real-time systems
  • Passion for craft and attention to detail in everything you ship

Tech we use

TypeScript C++ Rust WebSockets PostgreSQL Redis Kubernetes gRPC

Comp & benefits

Base salary: $185,000 - $235,000
Plus equity, 401k match, health/dental/vision, unlimited PTO, $1,000/month wellness stipend, annual team offsites.
