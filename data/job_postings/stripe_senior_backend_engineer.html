Senior Backend Engineer

San Francisco, CA (Hybrid) Payments Infrastructure Full-time

About the Role

We're looking for a Senior Backend Engineer to join our Payments Infrastructure team. You'll work on building and scaling systems that handle millions of requests per day, collaborating with a world-class engineering team.

What You'll Do

  • Design and build highly available, scalable backend services
  • Own end-to-end delivery of features from design to production
  • Collaborate closely with product, design, and infrastructure teams
  • Mentor junior engineers and contribute to engineering culture
  • Participate in on-call rotations and incident response
  • Write technical design documents and drive architectural decisions

What We're Looking For

  • 5+ years of professional software engineering experience
  • Strong proficiency in one or more: Python, Go, Java, Ruby
  • Experience designing and operating distributed systems at scale
  • Solid understanding of relational databases and data modeling
  • Track record of delivering complex projects with ambiguous requirements
  • Excellent communication skills and collaborative mindset

Nice to Have

  • Experience with payments or financial systems
  • Familiarity with event-driven architectures (Kafka, RabbitMQ)
  • Contributions to open-source projects
  • Experience with infrastructure-as-code (Terraform, Pulumi)

Compensation

The annual US base salary range for this role is $190,000 - $240,000. Total compensation also includes equity, benefits, and potentially a sign-on bonus.

Benefits

  • Health, dental, and vision insurance
  • 401(k) with company match
  • Flexible PTO
  • $10,000 annual learning & development budget
  • Home office stipend
  • Commuter benefits
